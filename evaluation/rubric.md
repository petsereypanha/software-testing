| Score | Criteria |

## Certificate Eligibility Requirements

All of the following must be met to receive any certificate:

- [ ] Attendance ≥ 80% of scheduled sessions
- [ ] All 10 weekly deliverables submitted (may be late, but must be submitted)
- [ ] Capstone project complete (all 7 artifacts present)
- [ ] Final presentation delivered
- [ ] Final score ≥ 70 / 100
- [ ] No major conduct violations
